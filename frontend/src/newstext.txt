Near term Inflation woes dragged domestic equity marketsInflation and rate hike news dominated domestic as well as global equity markets this week. News related to potential rate hike by US Fed weighed heavy on investors sentiments. Also, RBI’s FOMC meet minutes indicated a possibility of a rate hike as India’s inflation remained elevated, which further dampened investor sentiments. As a result, the broad-based Nifty 50 index fell by (-) 0.2%. The Small and Mid-Caps outperformed the broader market. The Nifty Midcap 150 Index gained 1.7% and the Nifty Smallcap 250 Index gained 1.9% this week.Sectoral indices were a mixed bag with Nifty IT, Nifty Banks and Nifty Consumer Durables gaining the most i.e., 1.0%, 0.9% and 0.6% respectively. While Nifty Pharma, Nifty Healthcare and Nifty Oil and Gas fell the most losing (-) 1.6%, (-) 1.3% and (-) 1.2% respectively.
